"FAQ" - Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is RuralBiz AI?

RuralBiz AI is an AI-powered platform that helps rural entrepreneurs plan, start, and grow their businesses effectively.

2. Who can use this platform?

Anyone from rural areas looking to start or expand a business can use RuralBiz AI.

3. How do I get funding guidance?

Our platform provides insights into funding options, grants, and investment opportunities tailored to your business type.

4. Are the microplans personalized?

Yes, microplans are customized based on your village, business category, and market potential.

5. Can I access success stories?

Absolutely! You can view stories of other rural entrepreneurs who have benefited from our guidance.

6. Is training available?

Yes, we provide training modules and mentorship programs to help you improve your business skills.

7. How do I start using RuralBiz AI?

Simply sign up on the platform, fill in your business details, and get personalized guidance instantly.

8. Is there any cost involved?

Basic guidance is free. Certain advanced features or consultancy may have subscription plans.

9. Can I get support in local languages?

Yes, our platform supports multiple local languages for better accessibility.
